CEDAC

Centre Of Excellence for Developing Age Friendly Communities aims to enhance the quality of life for seniors through tailored programs and support services.

VAYOPRATHIBHA

VAYOPRATHIBHA focuses on empowering older adults with skills and resources to engage actively in society, promoting their dignity and independence.

SMRITHIYORAM

SMRITHIYORAM is dedicated to preserving the memories and experiences of seniors through storytelling and memory-sharing initiatives

VAYOSMITHAM

VAYOSMITHAM promotes holistic well-being for seniors, focusing on physical, mental, emotional, and oral health in a supportive community.

RESPECTAGE

RESPECTAGE advocates for the rights and dignity of older adults, ensuring they are treated with respect and compassion in all aspects of life.

>

VIRTUAL REALITY ZONE

The VR ZONE offers immersive experiences designed to engage seniors, enhancing their cognitive and emotional well-being through innovative technology.

DEMENTIA FRIENDLY CARE

DEMENTIA FRIENDLY CARE provides specialized services and support for individuals with dementia, fostering an understanding and inclusive community.

G-TOURISM

G-TOURISM promotes travel experiences tailored for seniors, encouraging exploration and adventure while ensuring accessibility and comfort.

3G AGRICULTURE

3G AGRICULTURE focuses on sustainable farming practices that involve seniors, promoting community involvement and environmental stewardship.

RESPIRE WELLNESS

RESPIRE WELLNESS merges traditional Kerala art forms like Thiruvathira and Kalari with physical exercise to boost balance, breath control, and coordination.

YUVAVIGYAN

YUVAVIGYAN fosters intergenerational learning and collaboration, encouraging youth and seniors to engage and learn from each other.

AKSAM

AKSAM offers vital resources and support for seniors, enhancing their health and well-being through statewide sports meets and comprehensive care

MANAGING AND GENERATING INCOME FOR COMMUNITY SERVICES

We’re the authorized agency behind Age-Friendly Kochi, part of the WHO Global Network of Age-Friendly Cities and Communities (GNAFCC), dedicated to creating inclusive, age-friendly spaces.
